01 — Logo

The mark & wordmark

The mark is a rising route ending at a destination marker — it signals both driving and earnings growth. Use the app-icon squircle for product/app contexts and the horizontal wordmark for documents, headers, and the web.

App icon (squircle)
Horizontal wordmark
Reversed (on red/dark)

Do

  • Keep clear space ≥ the height of the marker dot on all sides
  • Minimum size: 24px (icon), 120px wide (wordmark)
  • Use the reversed version on red or dark backgrounds

Don't

  • Recolor the mark outside the brand red / white pairing
  • Stretch, rotate, or add shadows to the wordmark
  • Place the squircle on a busy photo without contrast
