DriverLog Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

DriverLog ("the app") helps drivers track earnings, fuel, and driving insights. This policy explains what the app stores and how it is handled. DriverLog is designed to be private by default.

What we store

Guest mode: if you use the app as a guest, all your data (sessions, fuel entries, preferences) is stored only on your device in the browser's local storage. None of it leaves your device.

Account mode: if you create an account to sync across devices, we store:

  • your email address (to identify your account and let you log in), and
  • the driving data you enter — trip sessions, revenue, tips, fuel costs, distances, and app preferences.

Passwords are handled by our authentication backend (PocketBase) and stored as secure hashes — never in plain text. Your driving data is private to your account and is not readable by other users.

Where it is stored

Account data is stored on the DriverLog sync server operated by the app's creator. Data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. We do not sell your data.

Advertising

The app may display ads via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies or device identifiers to serve and measure ads. See Google's advertising policies for details and opt-out options. DriverLog does not share your account email or driving entries with advertisers.

Consent (EEA, UK & Switzerland). For visitors in these regions, ads follow Google's EU User Consent Policy. We use a Google-certified Consent Management Platform (integrated with the IAB Transparency & Consent Framework) to request your consent before any personalized ads or non-essential cookies are used; by default consent is set to "denied" until you choose. You can change or withdraw consent any time from the privacy / consent prompt, and manage Google ad personalization at myadcenter.google.com.

Your control

  • Export: download your sessions any time as a CSV file from Settings.
  • Delete an entry: deleting a session or fuel record removes it from your device and, for account users, from the sync server.
  • Delete your account/data: contact us (below) to have your account and all associated data permanently deleted.
  • Stay local: use guest mode to keep everything on-device.

Children

DriverLog is intended for working drivers and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
