## Full description

**EN**

```
The number your app shows at the end of a shift isn't your real profit.

DriverLog is a free shift log book for Grab, Lineman, Bolt, Shopee Food,
and taxi drivers — it tracks what you actually earn after fuel, so you
know which shifts, times, and platforms are really worth it.

WHAT IT DOES
• Log each trip or shift — provider, distance, fuel, revenue, tips
• Automatic net-profit calculation, no manual math
• Live shift timer: start a shift, log each drop-off, end shift — the
  session fills itself in
• Fuel log with cost-per-liter tracking
• Dashboard: net revenue, fuel cost ratio, revenue per trip, earnings
  trend, best time to drive, top earning days
• Vehicle maintenance log — never miss a service
• Export everything to CSV any time

BUILT FOR HOW YOU ACTUALLY DRIVE
• Works fully offline — log a shift with no signal, it syncs when you're
  back online
• Free account syncs your log across every device
• Log in with LINE, or email — whichever's faster
• Thai and English, switch anytime
• Light and dark mode

RUN A FLEET?
Invite drivers to your fleet and see aggregated revenue, net profit, and
distance across your whole team — read-only, drivers keep full ownership
of their own data, opt-in only.

FREE, NO CATCH
No subscription, no ads inside the app, no paywalls on core features. If
DriverLog is helping you track your money, buying the developer a coffee
is appreciated but never required.

Built by a small team, for drivers who'd rather know their numbers than
guess.
```
